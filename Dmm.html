About DMM

Disciples Making Movement (DMM) is a spiritual movement focused on developing followers of Jesus Christ who make other disciples. It emphasizes prayer, Bible study, peace, love, and leadership development to transform lives and communities.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To see communities transformed through disciples who live like Christ.

Mission: To spread the Gospel, build strong believers, and train leaders who disciple others.
